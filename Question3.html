Web accessibility ensures that websites are usable by people with disabilities, including those with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive impairments.

Importance of Accessibility:

Ensures equal access to information and functionality. Improves user experience for all users, including those using assistive technologies. Helps websites comply with legal standards (like WCAG). Enhances SEO and performance, benefiting all users.

User Groups Benefited:

People with visual impairments (screen readers, color contrast). Users with motor disabilities (keyboard navigation). Individuals with hearing impairments (captions, transcripts). People with cognitive disabilities (simple language, clear navigation).
